Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Introduction

Welcome to SleepCycle.io. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our sleep cycle calculator website.

Important Note: SleepCycle.io is designed to work entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal sleep data to our servers. Your calculations remain private on your device.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website

Information You Provide

SleepCycle.io does not require you to provide any personal information to use our sleep cycle calculator. If you choose to contact us, we may collect your email address and any information you voluntarily provide.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our website
  • To analyze website usage and improve our services
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To ensure the security of our website
  • To comply with legal obligations

Sleep Data Privacy

Your sleep calculations and personal sleep data are processed locally in your web browser and are never transmitted to our servers. We do not store, track, or analyze your personal sleep patterns. Your sleep information remains completely private to you.

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized usage statistics to improve our service
  • No personal data collection through our calculator

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (anonymized data only)
  • Content Delivery Networks for faster page loading
  • Font providers (Google Fonts)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Since our calculator works entirely in your browser, your personal sleep data never leaves your device.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

SleepCycle.io may be accessed from around the world. If you are accessing our website from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:
